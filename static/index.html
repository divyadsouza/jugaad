The Story

In 2050, Earth is gasping. The air tastes like rust. The oceans have turned grey. The forests exist only in memory — digital fragments preserved by machines that helped destroy them. But from the rubble rises JUGAAD — the ancient art of making something from nothing. Armed with recycled tech and raw determination, the last generation fights back. Not with armies. With creativity.
